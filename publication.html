  • School Magazine Co-Editor (2021):

    During my high school years, I had the privilege of co-leading a talented group of students in publishing our school's very first magazine. In this role, I served as the co-editor, co-designer, and co-writer. The experience was transformative, as I learned the art of design and visual storytelling, using tools like Canva to create captivating layouts, harmonizing colors, and integrating imagery. As a writer, I took the initiative to organize a dedicated student committee responsible for collecting data from all school departments, capturing images of various events and student life, gathering student opinions and feedback on school events and achievements, surveying parents about the quality of education, and engaging in constructive discussions with teachers about school improvements. Beyond honing my design and writing skills, this magazine project taught me invaluable lessons about teamwork, leadership, active listening, and effective communication. Copies of our magazine were prominently displayed in the registration office and the principal's office, garnering recognition and praise, including commendations from the Ministry's Board of Education during their visit.

  • Online Debate and Book Clubs Creator (2021):

    During a challenging period of quarantine, I took the initiative to establish online debate and book clubs for in-school students, under the guidance of my English and Communication teacher. This endeavor aimed to make the most of our time in quarantine and foster learning and communication. The impact was significant, as several parents expressed their gratitude for the positive transformations they observed in their children. Students who joined these clubs gained confidence in expressing themselves in English, shared their thoughts and opinions with courage, and expanded their knowledge across various topics. This experience reinforced the value of community and the power of knowledge sharing.

  • Children's Club Creator (2021):

    As an active member of the Children's Club at school, I had the opportunity to deepen my understanding of child psychology. This experience allowed me to connect with younger students, learn about their needs and interests, and contribute to a supportive and nurturing educational environment. It underscored the importance of empathy and patience in working with children and broadened my perspective on educational dynamics.

  • Website Design Competition (2020):

    I was part of a dynamic team that achieved first place in a school website design competition. This competition brought together students from grades 7 to 12, challenging us to create a website focused on a food show called "Safdariyan Food." Our collaborative effort resulted in a captivating website featuring sections for donations, blogs, contact information, and a homepage. This experience taught me the power of teamwork and the potential for outstanding results when diverse talents and ideas come together.

  • International Owlypia Achievements (2020-2021):

    Over the course of two years, I actively participated in various U.S. international Olympiads, where I achieved notable success. I earned three bronze medals, one silver medal, and multiple participation certificates across diverse disciplines, including Economics and Business, Science and Technology, and Arts and Design. These achievements not only showcased my dedication to academic excellence but also highlighted my ability to excel in a range of subjects.
